# Elsevier Shared Family Policy

## Can This Family Template Be Reused?

Yes, as a drafting and preview baseline.

## What May Be Shared

- the archive zip stored at the family root
- the `template-package/` entry root
- the primary family class or sample manuscript
- the baseline float, reference, and front-matter rhythm expected by the family

## What Still Needs Journal-Level Confirmation

The Elsevier family package can be reused as a baseline across many Elsevier journals, but citation mode, front matter, and elsarticle versus CAS choice still need journal-specific confirmation.

## Safe Rule

Use elsarticle or CAS as the family baseline, then match the target journal's citation mode and front matter.
