# Springer Nature Shared Family Policy

## Can This Family Template Be Reused?

Yes, as a drafting and preview baseline.

## What May Be Shared

- the archive zip stored at the family root
- the `template-package/` entry root
- the primary family class or sample manuscript
- the baseline float, reference, and front-matter rhythm expected by the family

## What Still Needs Journal-Level Confirmation

The Springer Nature package can be reused as a family-level baseline across Springer, Nature Portfolio, and related titles that share the sn-jnl workflow, but title-specific style options still need confirmation.

## Safe Rule

Use sn-jnl as the family baseline, then tighten the target title's style option and author guide.
