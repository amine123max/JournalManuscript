# IOP Shared Family Policy

## Can This Family Template Be Reused?

Yes, as a drafting and preview baseline.

## What May Be Shared

- the archive zip stored at the family root
- the `template-package/` entry root
- the primary family class or sample manuscript
- the baseline float, reference, and front-matter rhythm expected by the family

## What Still Needs Journal-Level Confirmation

The IOP package can be reused as a family-level baseline with caution, but some IOP titles do not require authors to submit in that exact class format.

## Safe Rule

Use the IOP package as the family baseline with caution, and let the target title guidance win when it diverges.
