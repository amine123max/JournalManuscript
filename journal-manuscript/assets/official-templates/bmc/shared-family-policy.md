# BMC Shared Family Policy

## Can This Family Template Be Reused?

Yes, but through the shared Springer Nature package root rather than an independently downloaded BMC family archive.

## What May Be Shared

- the shared `template-package/` root
- the Springer Nature `sn-jnl` class workflow used by BMC titles in this repository
- the family-level baseline for front matter, section flow, and bibliography options

## What Still Needs Journal-Level Confirmation

BMC titles still need journal-specific confirmation for article type, reporting requirements, title-page metadata, and bibliography settings.

## Safe Rule

Use the shared Springer Nature package root as the BMC family baseline, then tighten the manuscript against the live BMC title guidance before calling anything submission-ready.
