# Wiley Shared Family Policy

## Can This Family Template Be Reused?

Yes, as a drafting and preview baseline.

## What May Be Shared

- the archive zip stored at the family root
- the `template-package/` entry root
- the primary family class or sample manuscript
- the baseline float, reference, and front-matter rhythm expected by the family

## What Still Needs Journal-Level Confirmation

The local Wiley package can be reused as a family-level baseline across Wiley journals, but journal-level front matter and bibliography requirements still need manual confirmation.

## Safe Rule

Use the Wiley package as the family baseline, then tighten journal-level front matter and bibliography requirements.
