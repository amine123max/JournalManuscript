# ACM Shared Family Policy

## Can This Family Template Be Reused?

Yes, as a drafting and preview baseline.

## What May Be Shared

- the archive zip stored at the family root
- the `template-package/` entry root
- the primary family class or sample manuscript
- the baseline float, reference, and front-matter rhythm expected by the family

## What Still Needs Journal-Level Confirmation

The ACM package can be reused as a family-level baseline across ACM journals, but journal code, submission mode, metadata blocks, and bibliography settings still need title-specific confirmation.

## Safe Rule

Use acmart as the family baseline, then tighten the target journal code and submission mode.
