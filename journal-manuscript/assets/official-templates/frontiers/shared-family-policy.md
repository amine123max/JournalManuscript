# Frontiers Shared Family Policy

## Can This Family Template Be Reused?

Yes, as a drafting and preview baseline.

## What May Be Shared

- the archive zip stored at the family root
- the `template-package/` entry root
- the primary family class or sample manuscript
- the baseline float, reference, and front-matter rhythm expected by the family

## What Still Needs Journal-Level Confirmation

The Frontiers package can be reused as a family-level baseline across Frontiers journals, but specialty labels, declarations, and reporting sections still need title-specific confirmation.

## Safe Rule

Use the Frontiers package as the family baseline, then tighten specialty and reporting sections.
