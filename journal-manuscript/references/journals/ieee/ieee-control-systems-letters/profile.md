## Refined Format Notes

- Base class: `IEEEtran` in journal mode
- Expected presentation: concise letter-style control article with abstract and `Index Terms`
- Citation mode: numeric IEEE references
- Figure and table handling: one strong system or block-diagram figure and one compact quantitative table usually communicate the first-page story best
- Front matter focus: control problem, key theorem or design idea, and quantitative benefit should appear immediately

## Journal-Specific Emphasis

- Control theory, control design, systems analysis, and short high-impact letters

## Local Preview Tuning

- Inherit the IEEE two-column preview baseline
- Keep the manuscript denser and more compact than a full transactions article
- Favor concise wording, a narrow table, and a letter-like technical rhythm
