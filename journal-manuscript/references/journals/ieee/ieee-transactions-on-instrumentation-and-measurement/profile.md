## Refined Format Notes

- Base class: `IEEEtran` in journal mode
- Expected presentation: compact two-column instrumentation article with abstract and `Index Terms`
- Citation mode: numeric IEEE references
- Figure and table handling: measurement setups, calibration diagrams, and uncertainty or benchmark tables should be concise and technically clear
- Front matter focus: sensing principle, measurement pipeline, calibration strategy, and quantitative validation should appear early
