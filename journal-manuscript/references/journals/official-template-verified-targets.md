## Interpretation

- `official-family-template` means the preview is rendered with the official family class available for that publisher ecosystem, such as `IEEEtran`, `acmart`, `achemso`, or `revtex4-2`.
- `official-template-package` means the preview is rendered from an imported official template package stored in the local asset library.
- `blocked-missing-template-asset` means the journal could not be honestly verified yet because the official template asset is not available in the current environment.
